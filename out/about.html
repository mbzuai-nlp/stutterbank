About StutterBank

We are dedicated to advancing research and providing comprehensive support for individuals affected by stuttering. Through evidence-based research, innovative therapy approaches, and community support, we're improving lives worldwide.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Guided by our core values, we work to transform understanding and treatment of stuttering

Our Mission

StutterBank is dedicated to advancing research and providing comprehensive support for individuals affected by stuttering. We believe that through evidence-based research, innovative therapy approaches, and community support, we can improve the lives of millions of people worldwide.

Our Vision

We envision a world where stuttering is fully understood, effectively treated, and where individuals with stuttering feel supported, confident, and empowered in their communication.

Research Excellence

Our Research Focus

Our research spans multiple areas critical to understanding and treating stuttering

Early Childhood Intervention

Identifying and treating stuttering in its earliest stages, when intervention can be most effective.

Neural Mechanisms

Uncovering the brain mechanisms underlying stuttering through advanced neuroimaging techniques.

Therapy Outcomes

Rigorously evaluating the effectiveness of different therapy approaches with scientific evidence.

Technology Integration

Exploring how technology can enhance therapy delivery and support practice tracking.

Our Approach

We bring together diverse expertise to advance stuttering research and support

Speech-Language Pathologists

Experts in fluency disorders with deep clinical experience and research expertise.

Neuroscientists

Studying the brain mechanisms of speech and communication to understand stuttering at its core.

Psychologists

Understanding the psychological aspects of stuttering and its impact on individuals and families.

Technology Experts

Developing innovative solutions to enhance therapy delivery and accessibility.

Community Advocates
